New York Giants News

Jones Comes up Big in Road Win

After the Giants entered the game after a rough 0-3 start to the season, Daniel Jones stepped up for his squad this past Sunday against the Saints, throwing for over 400 yards and 2 touchdowns. The Giants offense got going in the second half, and they looked unstoppable. Barkley ran in the game winner in OT to give the Giants their first win on the season.

Eli Manning Ring of Honor Induction

At the half during the Giants week 3 matchup against the Saints, the Giants retired Manning's number and added his name to the ring of honor at MetLife.

Game Balls

Birthday Boy

The Giants victory over the Saints was a nice birthday gift for Jabrill Peppers, who was celebrating his birthday this past Sunday. The Giants were able to pick up their first win of the season as a birthday gift for Peppers.
